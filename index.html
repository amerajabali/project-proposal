Application Overview

SnapStudy is a fun, collaborative flashcard and quiz-making app designed for students who want to study alone or with friends in real time. Users can create decks, take quizzes, and even compete with others in study challenges. It's perfect for quick sessions before exams or group study nights!

Technology Overview

  • Languages: HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Libraries: React.js, Express.js, Socket.IO
  • Platform: Node.js, MongoDB
  • Hosting: Render.com (Backend + Frontend)
  • Source Code: GitHub Repository
